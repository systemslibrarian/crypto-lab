Museum, Lab, Playground

Real crypto, made legible.

A browser-native collection of cryptography demos, reference tools, and historical exhibits. The point is not decorative cyber UI. The point is to make hard ideas visible: deniable encryption, zero-knowledge, private retrieval, post-quantum primitives, stream ciphers, and the older systems that shaped them.
